# Scoring Rubric

This rubric helps instructors assess lab completion without reviewing spoilers or exploit steps.

## Criteria

- Setup verified: App runs locally and basic navigation works.
- Evidence quality: Clear screenshots and concise observations.
- Learning reflection: Student explains what they learned and why it matters.
- Ethics and responsibility: Student follows the no-spoilers policy.

## Suggested scale

- 0 = missing
- 1 = incomplete
- 2 = meets expectations
- 3 = exceeds expectations
