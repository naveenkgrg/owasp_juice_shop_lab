# Discussions Guidelines

If GitHub Discussions are enabled, use them for questions about setup, lab workflow, and general OWASP Top 10 concepts.

## Allowed topics

- Environment setup and troubleshooting (no spoilers)
- Clarifications about bootcamp workflow and submission
- High-level learning questions about OWASP Top 10 categories

## Not allowed

- Solutions, exploit payloads, or step-by-step instructions
- Links to public solutions or walkthroughs
- Posting sensitive or personal data
